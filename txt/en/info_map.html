Map projections

What is a map?

A map projection is a flat representation of the surface of the Earth. There are infinitely many ways of representing the Earth on a plane, but all of them will distort its appearance in some way. There is no perfect projection, and choosing one or another will depend on the purpose of your map.

With this program we can compare different map projections, and explore their geometric properties.

  • Select one projection from the list.
  • Select one of the four tools (Tissot's indicatrix, geodesic, loxodrome, aspect).
  • Choose the map appearance in the View menu.
  • Move and click the map to explore.
